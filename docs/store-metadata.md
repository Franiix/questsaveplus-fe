# Store Metadata Draft

## App Store / Google Play

### App name

`QuestSave+`

### Subtitle / short description

Track your gaming backlog and discover your next favorite game.

### Promotional description

Discover games, explore detailed IGDB-powered game pages, and keep your personal backlog organized in one place.

### Full description

QuestSave+ helps you discover video games and keep track of the titles you want to play, are currently playing, or have already completed.

With QuestSave+ you can:

- search and discover games through a curated catalog powered by IGDB
- open detailed game pages with release info, media, related titles, and extra metadata
- build and manage your personal backlog
- rate played games and keep personal notes
- update your profile and personalize your account with an avatar
- browse the app in Italian or English

QuestSave+ is designed to keep discovery fast and backlog management clear, mobile-friendly, and focused.

### Keywords

`games,backlog,video games,tracker,igdb,discovery,gaming,collection,wishlist`

### Category suggestions

- Primary: `Entertainment`
- Secondary: `Lifestyle` or `Utilities`

## What To Prepare Outside The Repo

### Screenshots

Prepare at least:

- iPhone 6.7"
- iPhone 6.5" or 6.1"
- Android phone

Suggested capture flow:

1. Home discovery
2. Search results
3. Game detail
4. Backlog screen
5. Profile screen

### Support URL

You need a public support URL, not only an email address.

### Privacy Policy URL

You need a public hosted privacy policy URL. The in-app legal screen is useful, but store submission normally requires a public web URL.

### Marketing copy

Prepare:

- release notes for `0.0.1-alpha`
- store listing images
- app icon validation on Apple and Google portals
